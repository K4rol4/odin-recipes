lasagna

Description

A recipe from my home in Emilia-Romagna, Italy. This lasagne verde made with spinach pasta cannot be beaten. It's excellent if you want to impress some guests — or even yourself. It may be a bit time-consuming, but it's well worth the TLC you put into it.

Ingredients

  • 5 ounces spinach - rinsed, stemmed, and dried
  • ⅝ cup semolina flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Make pasta: Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water to just below the bottom of the steamer.
  2. Make ragu: In a large skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat.
  3. While ragu is simmering, make béchamel: Combine butter and flour in a medium saucepan over medium-low heat.
