Sugar Salad

Description

A very simple oil and vingegar mixture to liven up your mixed greens! I used olive oil vs. veg. oil and cut back on the sugar. I also added a little salt and cracked black pepper.

Ingredients

  • Â¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 head green leaf lettuce, torn into bite-size pieces

Steps

  1. Whisk the vegetable oil, vinegar, and sugar in a small bowl. Place the lettuce in a large bowl. Drizzle the oil mixture over the lettuce and toss to coat.
