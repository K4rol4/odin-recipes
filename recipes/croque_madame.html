Croque Madame

Description

A classic croque madame makes the perfect French-inspired brunch or lunch dish. Once you try this, it will be hard to have "just" a ham and cheese sandwich again.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons salted butter, softened, divided
  • 1 ½ tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 ounces shredded Gruyère cheese
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
  2. Melt 1 1/2 tablespoons butter in a saucepan over medium-low heat. Whisk in flour and stir until slightly brown, about 3 minutes.
  3. While ragu is simmering, make béchamel: Combine butter and flour in a medium saucepan over medium-low heat.
